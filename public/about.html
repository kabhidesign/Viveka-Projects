Our Journey in Construction, Your Path to Satisfaction
2010

Construct Founding

The company is founded by Alfred
Smith, an experienced architect with a
passion for sustainable design.

2013

The First Project

The company completes its first major
project, a LEED-certified office building
that sets a new standard for
environmentally friendly design.

2016

Diversification

The company expands its services to
include interior design, and hires its first
interior designer.

BuildCraft Construction is a leading construction company dedicated to delivering exceptional results in every project. With over 20 years of experience in the industry, we have established ourselves as a trusted name known for our commitment to quality, craftsmanship, and customer satisfaction.Our team consists of experienced project managers, skilled craftsmen, and industry experts who are dedicated to delivering superior craftsmanship and innovative solutions.
